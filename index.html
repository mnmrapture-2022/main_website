ABOUT

RAPTURE 2K22

Yes! You read it right, After 2 long years we are extremely happy to announce our cultural fest Rapture 2k22. Our inter-collegiate cultural is named ‘Rapture’ meaning a feeling of extreme happiness. College cultural events are a memorable part of every student’s life where extravagant decorations, sizzling celebrities, public figures and most importantly, many fun-driven events embellish the day. Rapture is all about having students from different colleges to create unforgettable memories and explore their talents along the way. We welcome you to participate and witness the grand events!
